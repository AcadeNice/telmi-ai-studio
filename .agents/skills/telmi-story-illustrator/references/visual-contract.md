# Telmi visual contract

## Blocking checks

- The requested story subject and action are visually present.
- No text, letters, digits, logos, watermarks, signatures, glyphs, runes, signs, labels, speech bubbles, or text-like decoration appear.
- The content is suitable for a young child and contains no frightening, violent, sexual, hateful, or otherwise adult material.
- Important faces, bodies, props, and actions remain inside a horizontal 4:3 crop-safe composition.
- Multi-choice images leave the bottom-left indicator zone visually quiet and unobstructed.
- A supplied reference preserves character identity and graphical style without copying an unrelated background or pose.

## Continuity hierarchy

Apply constraints in this order:

1. Explicit scene or choice action.
2. Canonical reference image identity and style.
3. Story-wide character, universe, palette, lighting, and medium descriptions.
4. Tasteful minor details that do not introduce new characters or plot elements.

## Small-screen guidance

- Prefer one clear focal action and a limited number of subjects.
- Use readable silhouettes, moderate contrast, and uncluttered backgrounds.
- Avoid tiny narrative details whose meaning disappears at 640 x 480.
- Do not add borders, cards, controls, arrows, captions, or interface elements.
- Exception: for a multi-choice asset, reserve the bottom-left corner; the application overlays its standardized navigation indicator after generation.
